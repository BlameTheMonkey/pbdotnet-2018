Welcome to John Andrews Counseling

I have been working with clients in a variety of settings for the past twenty years. My belief is that all people are capable of improving their quality of life by increasing insight to their thinking and increasing awareness of their behaviors. I believe that many people develop habits of thinking and behavior and often are not aware whether these habits are leading to happy, healthy living. My hope in working with clients is to help them identify, challenge and change ineffective patterns of thinking and behavior resulting in better emotional coping, healthier relationships, and a higher quality of life. Whether you struggle with addictive disorders, mental illness, patterns of unhealthy relationships, or difficult life circumstances counseling can help. I strive to provide a safe, supportive and non-judgmental environment for clients to pursue their counseling goals.

Scope of Practice

Individual therapy with adolescents and adults experiencing depression, anxiety, Post-traumatic Stress Disorder, Relationship Problems and difficult life events.

At your first appointment I will complete an assessment to learn about you and your life history and what brings you in for help. The first couple of sessions will be an opportunity to see if my counseling approach is a fit for you.
