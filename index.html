Services Offered

I provide website design, development and hosting for local small businesses. I concentrate on “brochure” websites giving your small business an online presence detailing your services or retail offerings, location and contact information.

“I Make Websites So You Don't Have To”

Today you need a web presence in order to be found by prospective customers. A lot of people, myself included, do not have a land line phone, so don't receive a phone book, hence no yellow pages. Various online directories provide only basic information on an unattractive page surrounded by advertisements.

Website

I can provide a stylish, up-to-date website design that will look great on any device; computer, tablet or phone.

Hosting

I provide hosting for your website that includes any maintenance, updates or adjustments required.

SEO

SEO, or search engine optimization. Your website will be submitted to all the major search engines; Google, Yahoo and Bing. In addition, appropriate measures will be taken within the websites code to facilitate proper search engine results.

Gundog Kennels

Naturally, with an operation like Gundog Kennels, the emphasis is on the dogs, and, as with the original website, there isn't much textual content. As Rodger said, “visitors want to see the dogs”. So this design is heavy on the display of photos, starting with the home page which is made up entirely of one large photo.

Be sure to keep an eye on the “For Sale” page for the availability of pups when there is a new litter.
